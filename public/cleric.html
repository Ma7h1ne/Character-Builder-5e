Cleric Features

Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 4) + your Constitution modifier per cleric level after 1st

Spellcasting Ability

As a conduit for divine power, you can cast cleric spells. At 1st level, you know three cantrips of your choice from the cleric spell list. You have two, 1st level spell slots available at cleric level one. Unlike Wizards, clerics do not need a spellbook to store their spells, and are assumed to know every cleric spell their spell slots can afford them.

Divine Domain

Choose one domain related to your deity: Knowledge, Life, Light, Nature, Tempest, Trickery, or War. See the Player’s Handbook for details on all the domains.
Your choice grants you domain spells and other features when you choose it at 1st level. It also grants you additional ways to use Channel Divinity when you gain that feature at 2nd level, and additional benefits at 6th, 8th, and 17th levels.(Choose 1 below)

The Life domain focuses on the vibrant positive energy—one of the fundamental forces of the universe that sustains all life. The gods of life promote vitality and health through healing the sick and wounded, caring for those in need, and driving away the forces of death and undeath. Almost any non-evil deity can claim influence over this domain, particularly agricultural deities.
